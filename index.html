Player1 deals first,
q to deal a card, f to slap

SlapJack: when a Jack is on top of the central pile, winner takes the entire central pile.

Double/Pair: Two cards of the same number, winner takes the entire central pile.

Sandwich: Two cards of the same number,separated by a different card in the middle. winner takes the entire central pile.

If bad slap, player loses their top card to the opponent.

If one player loses all their cards, they have one more chance to SlapJack to stay in the game
